How It Works

1. Add Your Best Posts

Find 5-10 of your most successful past posts. Add them one by one using the form on the left.

2. Categorize Correctly

Select the correct "Type of Post". This teaches the AI what a "New Listing" post looks like versus a "Testimonial".

3. Generate New Content

Once you've added enough examples, go to the "Content Generator" page. The AI will now use your style to create new, on-brand posts.

What makes a good example?

  • Posts that got high engagement.
  • Content that truly represents your brand.
  • A variety of post types.
